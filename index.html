ABOUT

Darworms is a territory capture game played by one to four Darworms.

Darworms are creatures that crawl about on a hex grid. Each Darworm learns which direction to go for each of the possible configurations of the six spokes exiting the grid point it can encounter. The Darworm is either trained by a preset set of instructions, or trained by you by asking you which direction to go in every time it encounters a pattern of spokes it hasn't seen before.

The Darworm that captures the sixth and last spoke in any grid point captures that gridpoint.

You can generate a Darworm with a preset randomly chosen direction for each grid point spoke configuration or train a new Darworm yourself. It may take many games to completely train a Darworm since it may not see all possible grip point spokes configurations for many games.
