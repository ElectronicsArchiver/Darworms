ABOUT

Darworms is a territory capture game played by one to four Darworms.

Darworms are creatures that crawl about on a hex grid eating the grid lines. Darworms exit a grid point by eating one of the six grid lines exiting the point. If all six grid lines are eaten the Darworm starves to death. Darworms each contain a program instructing it which direction to go for each of the 64 possible grid line patterns (eaten or not) it can encounter. You train, or program, your Darworm by playing games. Each time your Darworm sees a new pattern of grid lines it stops and asks your which direction to go. It remembers this and adds it to its program. The next time it sees the same pattern of grid lines it will go in that same direction again without stopping to ask you which way to go. Soon your Darworm will take off on its own. It may take several games to completely train (program) your Darworm.

The Darworm that eats the sixth and last spoke in any grid point captures that grid point. You get one point for each grid point you capture.

You can generate a Darworm with a preset randomly chosen program or train a new Darworm yourself. You can play the same Darworm in successive games.
